A Simple Way to Pray

100% Free

A straightforward, guided online resource designed to help you build a consistent and meaningful prayer life. Now featuring a Catechism Memorization tool and a Prayer Wall — where users can submit, pull, and fulfill real prayer requests from the community.

Also includes in-depth confessional Lutheran studies on the Trinity and the Nicene Creed — deep theological resources wrapped in digital simplicity. Accessible anywhere, entirely for free.
