About Us

We're a small LLC with a single conviction: spiritual disciplines shouldn't require fighting through bad software to get there.

Our Mission

Removing barriers to spiritual disciplines through digital simplicity — ensuring the best tools stay out of your way so you can focus on what truly matters.

Our Identity

Proudly confessional Lutheran, we build with the historic creeds and catechisms as our foundation. Our tools are designed to serve the whole Church — grounded in the rich tradition of the Lutheran confession of faith.

Our Philosophy

Technology should reduce friction, not add it. Every product is evaluated by a simple standard: does it help you pray, study, and grow — or does it get in the way? If it's in the way, it doesn't ship.

Accessible to All

A Simple Way to Pray is, and will always remain, completely free — no subscriptions, no paywalls. Across all our products, accessibility is a design requirement, not an afterthought. The tools we build should be within reach for every congregation and individual who needs them.
